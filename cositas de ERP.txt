EPM - Enterprise performance management (EPM) software is an on-premise or cloud-hosted digital tool designed to collect and manage metrics related to financial performance and business planning processes

PLM - is the strategic process of managing the complete journey of a product from initial ideation, development, service, and disposal.

SRM - software centralizes supplier-related data, helps select fitting suppliers, analyzes their performance, and identifies related risks to improve supplier collaboration planning and management.

CRM - Customer relationship management is any tool, strategy, or process that helps businesses better organize and access customer data.

SCM - SCM software vastly improves the visibility of the entire supply chain process. It helps business operations make smarter decisions about inventory.

PRM - is a combination of the software, processes and strategies companies use to streamline business processes with partners who sell their products.

KM - Knowledge management software is a subset of enterprise content management software, which contains a range of software that specializes in the way information is collected, stored and/or accessed.

BI - is a type of application software designed to retrieve, analyze, transform and report data for business intelligence.

B2B -  is a way of doing commerce, specifically companies doing business with other companies.

B2C - refers to the process of selling products and services directly between a business and consumers who are the end-users of its products or services.

B2A - enables internet-based transactions between companies and government entities.

POS - allows your business to accept payments from customers and keep track of sales.

TPV - Third-party verification is a process of getting an independent party to confirm that the customer is actually requesting a change or ordering a new service or product.
